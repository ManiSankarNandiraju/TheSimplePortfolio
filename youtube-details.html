Cover Songs

As someone who has an unshakable love for hip hop, I've found my sanctuary in singing and rapping – it's where I discover my confidence and a euphoric sense of self-expression. With a deep-rooted appreciation for films and the art of storytelling, I've embarked on a journey to create my own unique blend of art. I currently create cover songs of hip hop tracks using karaoke, and my future plans involve transforming these covers into full-fledged musical videos.I aim to enhance my storytelling abilities and share my passion for hip hop with the world, one verse at a time.

Gaming

Gaming has always been my stress-buster, and I cherish the daily moments spent with friends, conquering worlds in Fortnite, Minecraft, Genshin Impact, GTA 5, Need for Speed Most Wanted, and many more. We even dive into simple yet delightful web games. In my quest to share the joy of gaming, I've ventured into live streaming our daily adventures. Looking ahead, I'm planning to create highlights and funny edits of our gaming escapades. If you don't want to miss out on the fun, be sure to subscribe to my channel, and let's continue this incredible gaming journey together!

Short Films

Ever since I watched '1 Nenokkadine' during my 10th grade, I've been captivated by the art of filmmaking. The intricate process behind the scenes, the storytelling magic, it all fascinated me. I ventured into creating my own short film called 'Alone' during my graduation, and while I acknowledge it as an amateur piece, it's my film - a piece of my creative soul. I even started another short film during the pandemic, though it didn't make the final cut due to some disappointments with the frames. However, I'm determined to release it now, embracing the imperfections as part of the journey. This journey has taught me not to let self-doubt hold me back, and I'm eager to continue creating videos, no matter the outcome. I have a trove of ideas and scripts waiting to be developed, and I'm excited to see where this path takes me.
